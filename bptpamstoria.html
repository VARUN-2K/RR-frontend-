BPTP Amstoria

AMSTORIA - The township has arisen as a place like no other, 200 acres of openness dotted with luxurious Farm villas and country floors. BPTP Amstoria Gurgaon, offers you residential plots with sizes of (300 & 500) sq. yards. BPTP Amstoria residential project is itself a landmark in the midst of nature.

Bptp Amstoria residential project sector 102 offers you a clubhouse with delights like pool, jogging tracks, gymnasium, multi cuisine restaurant, shopping center, indoor/outdoor games, table tennis, squash courts, badminton court, banquet hall, exotic spa, business center services, water sport activities and many more. It has a wide, well planned streets lines with canopied trees. At life in this awesome dream home, you would never be far from the important places like schools, colleges, corporate offices, restaurants, hotels and many more.
