Omaxe Chandni Chowk

Omaxe is coming up with the retail mall at shopping paradise of Delhi The Project is located at adjacent to Chandni Chowk Metro Station (Violet Line) and opposite to Sis Ganj Gurudwara

OMAXE CHOWK has brought a new era to Chandni Chowk. This possibility of having largest & busiest shopping destination in the heart of City is going to take businesses to an all new level. Chandni Chowk sees 6–8 lakh Visitors everyday. As per JLL Report 40 Lakh Serious Shoppers would be visiting this place every month. This makes 7 times more footfall than any other mall in Delhi-NCR. Project spread across 4.5 acres with approx. 1.20 Lakh sq.ft. of Food Court area having 1600+ of seating, serving local delicacies & other cuisines. Parking for more than 2100 cars would be available on three basement floors & third floor.
